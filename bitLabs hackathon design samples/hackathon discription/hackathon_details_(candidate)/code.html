Innovate for Good Hackathon

Description

Join us for the Innovate for Good Hackathon, a 48-hour event focused on developing technology solutions that address pressing social and environmental challenges. Participants will work in teams to create innovative projects that align with the United Nations' Sustainable Development Goals. This hackathon is open to developers, designers, and innovators of all skill levels who are passionate about making a positive impact through technology.

Rules

Participants must form teams of 2-5 members. All projects must be original and developed during the hackathon. Use of pre-existing code or assets is allowed, but the majority of the work must be completed within the 48-hour timeframe. Teams must submit a project demo and a brief presentation outlining their solution, its impact, and its alignment with the Sustainable Development Goals.

Judging Criteria

Projects will be judged based on innovation, impact, technical execution, and presentation quality. Judges will evaluate how well the solution addresses a specific Sustainable Development Goal, its potential for real-world application, and the creativity and effectiveness of the team's approach.
